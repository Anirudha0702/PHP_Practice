Distinctiveness

n keeping with the mission of the college we strive towards inclusiveness and equity. The administration ensures that all stakeholders are provided with a level playing field and special care is taken to address the needs of the physically- challenged. The administration has taken extra pains to ensure that a physically-challenged student who is keen to continue his/her education is allowed to do so even though that meant getting special permission from the affiliating University. Different Depts. have started social outreach programmes by which they try to reach out to the sections of the society with special needs.

About Us

New Alipore College was set up on 5th August 1963 as a Government Sponsored College on a large plot of land owned by the Government of West Bengal. The only building of the then Day College, constructed by the Public Works Department, was three-storied. Turned into four-storied much later, it still remains the main building of the College.
